The Burger Triumvirate

Imagine, for a moment, that your daily intake of food was restricted -- severly restricted. This restriction would imply that you could consume nought but one type of food thrice a day. In time, this would degrade one's nutrition, nearly placing one in a survival situation. Now imagine, quite on the contrary, that this food was at the pinacle of globalized, leisurely society, where nearly any number of alternatives would be accessible. In this case, the food occurs in the form of hamburgers, a choice unfortunately not far from imagination in present society. In order to help enlighten people on the importance of nutrition, we have sought to simulate the consumption of three burgers a day, over one year, in an easy and understandable format.
